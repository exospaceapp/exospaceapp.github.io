Planet History

In the vast expanse of the cosmos, a unique event unfolded. A modest passenger from Earth, a bacterium, has embarked on an interstellar journey. This bacteria is the same species found on the Tanpopo mission which is an orbital astrobiology experiment investigating the potential interplanetary transfer of life, organic compounds, and possible terrestrial particles in the low Earth orbit, an ambitious project studying the possibility of life's existence beyond our home planet. Life

A sporoid is a spore-like creature that can travel through the air and colonize new habitats. It has a spherical shape and a thin membrane that allows it to float. It can sense radiation and move toward it by using flagella-like structures. It can also produce toxins and enzymes that help it break down organic matter and infect other creatures. Sporoids are parasitic and can take over the bodies of their hosts, turning them into zombie-like creatures.
